--- layout: post title: 'Scala: SBT OutOfMemoryError: PermGen space' date: '2013-09-01T13:23:00.003-07:00' author: Christopher Batey tags: - scala - permgen - sbt - jvm modified_time: '2013-09-01T13:23:45.741-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4161315644722406995.post-6616375303652024192 blogger_orig_url: http://christopher-batey.blogspot.com/2013/09/scala-sbt-outofmemoryerror-permgen-space.html --- When starting out with Scala/SBT I very quickly ran into perm gen issues followed by SBT crashing:

java.lang.OutOfMemoryError:
    PermGen space

Especially when running the console from within interactive mode.

To fix/brush this under the carpet add the following to your profile (.bashrc / .bash_profile) and source it again (run . ~/.bashrc) 

export
    SBT_OPTS=-XX:MaxPermSize=256m
